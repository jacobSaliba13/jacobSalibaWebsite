0.1 PROTO

As Proto 0.1 is ongoing project most of the designs of the actual “instruments” are being designed and tested as to see the limits and possible outcomes of certain shapes, materials, sound filters ecc.. The project was tested by two sound engineers which were trying to explore the possibilities of such as simple design yet with huge possibilities and outcome. Together with their help I am trying to explore these relationships and how this “object” can be redesigned as to fit into certain projects such as music production, sound scaping and immersive environments.

Currently I am in a testing phase where the first format of the “instrument was tested and explored using Ableton live, layering certain melodies as to compose certain sounds. The instrument was filtered through a number of settings with Ableton. Further development within this process will explore the use of Arduino and how certain effects can be independently done without the use extra software. Another element which is being explored is the possibility of gaining a MIDI output out of such an object which such data can be than transferred to both audio and visual software’s. Such exploration can lead to the creation of sound and visual from common objects such as rocks, water and plants, which can be further explored as to create new experiences within common environments
