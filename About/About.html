Biography

Jacob is a multi-media artist based in Malta. His passion for art started from a very young age yet he explored this interest further at the age of 16 whilst studying Art Aâ€™level and then furthering his studies at the University of Malta whilst reading for a degree in Fine Arts in Digital Arts. His interest is that of creating things; from paintings and drawings to generative art. His work is mainly based upon research and experimentation which has led him to evolve his work from traditional art to software-based art. The work explores the subjects of immersion, perception, emotion, social environments and music through tactile and immersive expressive work. He aims to create a dialogue between his work and the audience while making sure that his work encompasses an experience of its own.
