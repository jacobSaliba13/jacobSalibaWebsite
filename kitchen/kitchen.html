Kitchen

These images explore the underlying aspects of the kitchen and what goes on behind the restaurants back of house. The images aim at portraying workers and the reality of working in a stressful environment as that of a kitchen. "Kitchen" is a story line which covers the surrounding process and conversation during a service through human gestures. Through these photos we get an insight on the people who are behind doors and their daily life during and post service. The Project is inspired from one of the most fascinating kitchen documentary photography projects by Bob Carlos Clarke “White heat 25”
