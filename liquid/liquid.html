Liquid

Liquid is an ongoing project which I have exhibited together with Neil Grech in multiple places such as the opening of the Malta arts festival in 2019. The project explores methods from the 1960's audiovisual scene using the combination of light, water based colours and oil. The filming from this live experimentation is than inputted into VDMX and effects are synched with music with audio-anlayses.
