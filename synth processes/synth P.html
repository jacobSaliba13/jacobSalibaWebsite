Seeking Gesamtkunstwerk

Seeking Gesamtkunstwerk is my dissertation project, this project aims to answer and research two question, how to create an effective Audio -Visual performance through software? And what elements create immersion in an AV performance? To answer both of these questions I researched upon the principles of perception, immersion and the relationship between sound and visual. The research conclude that four main elements are the bases of any successful AV performance these are: Physical, Social Knowledge, Composing and Perception each category owns its very own sub categories which further explain the reasoning behind each category and why all of them are important as to create the concept of synchresis.

The research started by identifying what kind of software to use and why would one try to create customizable software when there are commercial VJ software which can be used. The video above shows some of the first tests done with Touch Designer. The notion of creating your own software was finally making sense to me the ability to create specific generative art which works exactly how you want it made the process fun and a new world was being presented to me.

The next step of the process was that to put my practice in a real live scenario. I created a VJ software which was made totally out of a procedural network for both the visual elements and the UI. This program was tested in a party where I video jocked for around 5 hours. The testing was priceless I could notice certain elements which were never thought off. This part motivated me further as to further my research and creat a software from which the musician could play both his music and visual all in real time.

The three clips above show the final software in work. Ableton and Touch designer were linked and data from the master program being Ableton was transferred to touch designer were all the visuals could be triggered and manipulated from such data automatically.

The final step of the processes was that of building an environment as for this project to be showcased in. The three renders above show the layout of the box which was built as to create the concept of immersion. The Box includes a short throw projector and a 360 speaker. The angle and distance of the screen was calculated as to create a 60-degree angle which is the humans average focal length angle. The box aims at disconnected people from their surroundings whilst immersing them in the audio-visual performance.
