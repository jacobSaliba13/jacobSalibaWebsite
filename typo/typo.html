Typo

Typo is a project which explores the notion of material vs meaning. The power of words and how these can be represented through material. Loving yourself and does around you is a difficult yet a powerfull qoute to live by. As to represent this I wanted to represnet this phrase in concrete a strong yet brittle material. This contrast represents this phrase as one could love his life and all there is around him yet is subject to more pain such as death, break ups, fights and social problems yet this love gives strenght which can build upon stronger structures and more happiness.
