Typo

Typo tries to communicate how success must be one that accomplishes both success on others and within an individual. This massage is represented through the materials used. Concrete was used as to convey such message. This material looks strong yet its brittle the same goes for someone who looks successful yet does not love himself causing him to be brittle inside. The concept was further explored in the location and colours chosen. The location shows an old abandoned hotel which represents somewhere where there was once success and now its all lost in the destroyed structures which is something that can be related to materialistic success which is temporary whilst if you love yours you can achieve the feeling of success in non-materialistic things which last forever. The use of colour was also important I choose the three primary colours which can all be related to a number of emotional states, red represents power and love, yellow represents fun, happiness and motivation and blue represents success and trust of one self.
